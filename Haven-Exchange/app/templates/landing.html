Travelers Exchange

A community economy for the No Man's Sky community. Earn coin for what you do in-game. Spend it on stuff other members make. That's it.

Get Started How It Works

You only need to know three things.

Everything else is optional. Start here.

1

What is this?

A community economy. You earn coin. You spend coin. No real money involved.

2

How do I get coin?

Play the game. Submit what you find. The system rewards you automatically.

3

How do I spend it?

Shops, stocks, or banks. Pick one. They're all in the menu when you log in.

The Loop

Here's how the whole thing works.

🎮
You play No Man's Sky
Just like normal. Explore, build, fight, whatever you do.
▼
📡
You submit your discoveries
Systems, ships, fauna, bases — whatever your community tracks.
▼
🤖
Keeper Bot rewards you
XP for the contribution + coin in your wallet, automatically.
▼
🛒
You spend your coin
Buy custom builds, region maps, mining services, whatever you need.
▼
🏪
Other members earn from you
The shop owners get the coin you spent. The cycle continues.
The simple version: Play → submit → earn → spend → other people earn → cycle continues. Forever.

Three places to spend.

Pick one, or all three. All optional.

🛒

Shops

Other members run shops. They sell custom builds, region maps, food buff menus, mining services, whatever they're good at.

📈

Stocks

Buy a piece of a nation or business. The price tracks how well it's doing. Sell whenever you want.

🏦

Loans

Need coin you don't have? Borrow from a bank or your nation's treasury. Hard cap on what you can ever owe.

Getting started takes 5 minutes.

Three steps. That's it.

1

Make your account

Pick a username and password. You'll have a wallet immediately.

2

Join your nation

Find your community in the Nations list. Apply. Wait for approval.

3

Start playing

Submit what you find in-game. Coin starts showing up.

Ready to join?

Registration is free. No real money involved. You can leave anytime.
